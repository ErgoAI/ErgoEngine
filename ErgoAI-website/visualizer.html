Flora-2 Visualizer

Visualizer Update Site

To install the Flora-2 Visualizer from the update site, you need Eclipse installed on your system. Start Eclipse and select "Software Updates" and then "Find and Install" from the Help menu. In the resulting dialog, select "Search for new features to install" and click "Next." In the next dialog, click on "New Remote Site" to insert this URL:

    http://flora.sourceforge.net/visualizer
